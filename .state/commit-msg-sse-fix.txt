fix(server): serialize SSE writes, replace polling with abort signal

- Add serial write queue (busy flag + pendingSnapshot) to handlePipelineStatus
  so concurrent writes never interleave SSE frames and errors on a closed
  stream are caught without unhandled rejections
- Replace 1-second polling waitUntilClosed with c.req.raw.signal abort event
  so the connection closes immediately on client disconnect with no polling overhead
- Remove now-unused waitUntilClosed helper
